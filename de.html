Data Engineering

NeST Digital provides turn-key innovative and customized data solutions leveraging our internal specialized data team and partners. We offer flexible engagement and commercial models to meet the needs of their clients.

Offerings

Data MVPs: Our highly skilled data engineers will help you build a minimum viable product (MVP) to demonstrate how to collect, clean, transform, store, analyze, and visualize data in the desired architecture.

Execution/delivery: With the internal talent, as well as with our partner organizations, we can take ownership of the delivery and execution of data projects.

Data consulting: We conduct a comprehensive assessment of your current data engineering and analytics platform and provide recommendations to build an organizational strategy that can extract value from your data.

Highlights

NeST Digital’s data engineering consultancy can provide expertise and experience in optimizing data infrastructure, improving efficiency by outsourcing tasks, reducing risk by identifying vulnerabilities, scaling data infrastructure to meet business needs, and providing access to emerging data technologies to drive innovation and gain a competitive advantage
